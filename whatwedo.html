OUR PLATFORM ALLOWS INDIVIDUALS TO MEET HIGHER GOALS MORE EFFECTIVELY AND GROW YOUR BUSINESS THE RIGHT WAY. HERE ARE 10 IMPORTANT QUESTIONS TO ASK ABOUT THE REAL ESTATE COMPANY OR FRANCHISE SYSTEM YOU ARE CURRENTLY WITH, OR CONSIDERING:

01

When a person is looking for you online, can they quickly get directly to you, your website, and phone number?

02

Can you do agent-to-agent referrals without going through the company’s relocation division? Do they generate referrals for you?

03

Does the company provide all levels of sales and business training - from the fundamentals of real estate sales all the way up to hiring staff, building a team, managing your expenses and creating a business?

04

Is the company clearly agent-centered? Does it understand that the consumer selects the agent, not the company?

05

Does the company know your personal and professional goals? Do they offer daily coaching and accountability to help you achieve those goals?

06

Does the company have a set of written beliefs and values that it shares and sincerely seeks to live by? Does the company have a spirit and culture of teamwork and positive energy?

07

How much are you spending on Cap? Does your company or team offer any reimbursement?

08

What lead protection are you offered? If you sell zero homes 2 months in a row, how do you afford to keep your lead stream consistent and abundant?

09

Does the company encourage collaboration and new ideas?

10

What transactional support do they offer? What’s their fee for that support? Is there someone to cover for you while on vacation?
